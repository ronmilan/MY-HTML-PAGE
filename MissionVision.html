Vision and Mission

Vision

PUP Santa Maria envisions to offer the students of the municipality
quality, relevant, responsive and excellent tertiary education aimed
to produce graduates that are future leaders and expert in their chosen
field of endeavor and contributes to the full development of the family,
community, socienty and the nation at large.

Mission

To provide high quality undergraduate programs and globally-competitive
human capital development and community services accessible particularly
to the students of the municipality.
